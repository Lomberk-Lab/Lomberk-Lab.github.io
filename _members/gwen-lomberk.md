Dr. Gwen Lomberk is an accomplished investigator whose research has advanced understanding of epigenetic and transcriptional mechanisms that govern cell identity, particularly in gastrointestinal biology and cancer. Her work has helped uncover how chromatin remodeling and gene regulatory networks contribute to disease development, and she is also a dedicated mentor committed to training the next generation of scientists in rigorous, collaborative research.
