Rebekah Fogarty received her Master’s degree in Life Sciences with a concentration in Biochemistry from Virginia Tech in 2024. Her work focuses on computational biochemistry and molecular dynamics simulations to understand the structural and functional impact of mutations in cancer. She is also interested in computational drug design approaches aimed at identifying and optimizing novel therapeutic strategies.
